Welcome to ARC Church

Our History

ARC Church was founded in 2000 with a vision to build a strong community of believers committed to worship, discipleship, and outreach.

We Come to Serve

Dedicated to serving God and people.

We are committed to helping others through acts of kindness, compassion, and faith-based service. Our church exists to be the hands and feet of Christ in our community.

Church Mission

To make disciples and spread God’s love.

Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ by teaching, equipping, and sending them to impact the world.

Church Belief

We believe in the Bible and the power of prayer.

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and that prayer is powerful and effective, connecting us directly to God’s presence and guidance.
